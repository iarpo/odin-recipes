Honey and Soy Chicken

Next level marinated chicken thighs.
On the plate in double time.

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • That's literally it

Put the chicken in a zip-lock with 40g of honey and 40g of soy sauce

After 20 minutes put it under a hot grill for about 5 minutes each side, or until the meat reaches 74°C, whichever comes first.
You have a limited window on this, so getting the grill nice and hot gives you some delicious crispy bits without overcooking the chicken.
