Enchiladas

Fiery and filling enchiladas.
Life affriming grub.

  • Meat - your choice of...
    • Beef mince (10% fat)
    • Chicken thighs
  • Wraps
  • Paprika
  • Black beans
  • Cheese
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Brown sugar
  • Onions or shallots

Concentrate on those earthy flavours - paprika, brown sugar, fat, black beans, caramelised onions.
Don't use haricot beans or bacon, they dilute the flavour down to nothing or make it taste mass produced.

Cut it with acid like lemon, lime or balsamic.

Get the sauce thick and rich. Make sure you taste the sauce after all the ingredients have been simmering and before you wrap up and get it in the oven.
Have fun with what you put in but beware, each ingredient takes its share of the sauce's flavour.
This is just chilli in a wrap, make sure your chilli's up to par first and remember that the sauce is king.

Leave the sauce off the ends of the wraps for some nice crunch.
